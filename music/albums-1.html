Perish Point

A remix of Lemon Demon's album Spirit Phone

Album cover, which incorporates elements from multiple iterations of Ming Doyle's Spirit Phone album art

Perish Point is a reworked version of Lemon Demon's 2016 album Spirit Phone created by me for the purpose of utilizing the stems and the many alternate versions of these songs available in order to create a fresh and unique experience for those familiar with the album.
