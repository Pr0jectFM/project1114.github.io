Primary Channel

My primary channel is where I post completed works, which is usually music. For my Sega Genesis music, I created a custom oscilliscope visualizer which shows what's going on on each channel, along with scrolling text that discusses the creation of the song. To accompany these songs, I've started drawing unique thumbnail art.

Secondary Channel

I created a second channel as an outlet for low effort and incomplete work. My most prominent type of content is song mashups, most often involving the layering of vocals from one song onto the instrumental of another. I enjoy creating these as a way of engaging with songs I enjoy beyond just listening to them.

Streams

I can often be found on streams hosted by my fellow RadiantNexus member LunarCryptik. It's usually on his YouTube channel, but sometimes it's on his Twitch channel. These streams usually involve Lunar playing games and discussing them with our friend group.

Off-Channel

My music for South Island Definitive and SHIMA have been uploaded to the RadiantNexus and Team Neo Kyukyoku YouTube channels respectively.

In 2022, I joined MarkeyJester on a short-lived podcast called "Hacker: Behind the Code". The premise is that Markey would interview various members of the Sonic hacking community, learning about their history with Sonic hacking and how that shapes the work they create.
